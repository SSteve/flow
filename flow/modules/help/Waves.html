A collection of harmonics drawn from the single-cycle waves of several classic synthesizers .

You can select harmonics for two sound sources, which are mixed. The harmonics are drawn from single-cycle waves of the Kawai K3, Ensoniq SQ-80, Prophet VS, and Casio CZ series. See the manual for more details.
